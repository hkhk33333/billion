The Solution

From Reports to Real-Time Treatment Intelligence

What Northstar Pulse Does

  • Longitudinal dashboard — Unified timeline of all Northstar results per patient, showing ctDNA burden trends alongside genomic changes
  • Trajectory prediction — ML models trained on treatment-response patterns flag early signs of resistance or response
  • Smart alerts — Proactive notifications when ctDNA levels deviate from expected trajectory post-therapy
  • Cohort benchmarking — Compare a patient's trajectory to de-identified outcomes from similar molecular profiles

Why Now

  • Data moat — BillionToOne's QCT technology + growing install base creates proprietary longitudinal dataset no competitor can replicate
  • Platform expansion — PGx and CH add-ons create richer multi-modal data per patient
  • Market timing — AI-enhanced liquid biopsy is the #1 trend in precision oncology (U.S. market: $2.78B in 2026, 13.8% CAGR)

Competitive Pressure

Guardant, Foundation Medicine, and Tempus are all building longitudinal analytics layers. If BillionToOne doesn't own this, superior sensitivity alone won't sustain differentiation.

Retrospective chart review in partner clinics; requires IRB approval

A/B comparison: Pulse users vs. non-Pulse matched cohort over 6 months

Why These Metrics

Adoption confirms product-market fit. Clinical utility validates that insights change behavior (not just engagement). Retention is the revenue proxy — if Pulse drives repeat testing, it directly grows Northstar ARR without new customer acquisition cost.
